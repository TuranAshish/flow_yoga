Your Questions, Answered

Everything you need to know before beginning your yoga and mindfulness journey with Flow Yoga.

🧘 Can beginners join?

Absolutely. Our programs are carefully designed for all experience levels, from complete beginners to advanced practitioners.

📅 How often should I practice?

We recommend 3–5 sessions per week. Consistency matters more than duration, and even 15 minutes daily can create noticeable benefits.

👴 Are classes suitable for seniors?

Yes. We offer gentle, low-impact practices designed to improve mobility, balance, and overall well-being.

🧠 Can yoga help with stress?

Yoga combines mindful movement, breathing techniques, and meditation practices that can help reduce stress and improve mental clarity.

💻 Do you offer online classes?

Yes. Join live online sessions from anywhere in the world and access guided content whenever you need it.

🎥 What happens if I miss a class?

Premium members receive access to recordings so they can practice anytime that fits their schedule.

❤️ Can yoga help with back pain?

Many students experience improved mobility and reduced discomfort through proper posture, stretching, and strengthening exercises.

⭐ Which plan should I choose?

Our Premium Plan is the most popular choice, providing access to all classes, meditation resources, and progress tracking tools.

👨‍🏫 Do you offer private coaching?

Yes. One-on-one sessions are available for personalized guidance, accountability, and faster progress.

🧘‍♀️ What do I need to get started?

Just a yoga mat, comfortable clothing, and a willingness to show up for yourself. We'll guide the rest.
